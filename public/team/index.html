Our Team

Ladi Delano

Ladi Delano has exceptional insight into company management that's enhancing the operations of Petraton. With his leadership, Petraton has established strong relations with the communities we operate in and with all governing and regulatory bodies to establish the company as preferred operator in the exploration and mining industry.

Ladi holds a Masters in Business Administration, a Masters in International & Public Affairs, and a Certification in Project Management. With over two decades of work experience, Ladi has broad experience in various industries including Oil and Gas, ICT, Banking, Media and Real Estate. His broad experience gives him unique flexibility and ingenuity to thrive in the ever evolving business landscape.
