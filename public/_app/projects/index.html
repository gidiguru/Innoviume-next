Our Projects

Petraton licenses are underlain by Archean rocks which are known to hold gold mineralization in both Nigeria and its conjugate Borborema trend (Brazil)

Gemstones such as beryl, precious metals like gold, and industrial minerals like tantalite, have been artisanally mined in the area.

A geological survey has identifed 10 pegmatite deposits. Grade and size of each deposit is being determined.

Our Products

Petraton licenses host several quartz and feldspar products which are important for the glass, ceramic, electronics, and battery industries.
